Piano lessons

By learning to play piano, you not only develop your creativity, concentration, patience or psychomotor coordination but you also increase your general well-being. Playing music has a therapeutic effect.
You can choose to sign up for 30 or 50 minute individual lessons provided both in-person and online. In person lessons take place at the Music & Mind Studio in Brussels (Etterbeek - Merode area).
My lessons are intended for:

  • Professional musicians and students who want to develop their skills or prepare an important audition/exam
  • Children starting or continuing their adventure with music
  • Adults, both beginners and those returning to their passion after many years

Please note that the number of available spots is limited. In case of luck of availability you can sign up for the waiting list.
