Performance Coaching

What can you expect from coaching?

Coaching raises self-awareness and identifies choices. It is the art of facilitating another person’s learning, development, well-being and performance.
Through coaching, people are able to find their own solutions and change their attitudes which may currently limit them in many different ways. The whole aim of coaching is to close the gap between people’s potential and their current state.

What are the most common topics brought on by my clients?

  • Tackling overwhelming performance anxiety (in music performance, arts and public speaking)
  • Managing time better
  • Finding the professional orientation that resonates more with what is meaningful to them
  • Learning how to turn ambitions into reality
  • Getting a better balance between work and home
  • planning an entry into a new job or profession
  • (re) defining the vision of their future

What makes coaching special to me?


Its multidisciplinary and holistic approach.
Origins of coaching have tangled roots in all of the following: psychology, therapy, psychiatry, medicine, physics, systems theory, hypnosis, management development, leadership theory, religion, philosophy - among others.
